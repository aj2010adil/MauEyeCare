1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide eye care services:

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Providing eye care consultations and treatments
  • Generating and managing prescriptions

  • Maintaining medical records for continuity of care
  • Appointment scheduling and follow-up care

3. WhatsApp Integration

We use WhatsApp Business API to:

  • Send prescription notifications to patients

  • Provide follow-up care instructions

Important: We only send messages related to your eye care treatment. We do not send promotional or marketing messages without explicit consent.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure database storage

  • Regular security updates and monitoring

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or legal process

6. Data Retention

We retain your medical records as required by medical practice regulations and law:

  • Medical records: As per local healthcare regulations
  • WhatsApp messages: Automatically managed by WhatsApp's retention policy

You have the right to:
